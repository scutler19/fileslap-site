## Quick Start: Your First PDF Conversion

No matter which platform you're using, getting started with FileSlap is simple:

### 1. Get Your API Key
Sign up at [fileslap.com](https://fileslap.com) and grab your free API key (50 conversions/month).

### 2. Make Your First Request
```bash
curl -X POST https://api.fileslap.com/api/convert \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "X-API-KEY: YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -d '{"html": "<h1>Hello World</h1>"}' \
  --output hello.pdf
```

### 3. Choose Your Platform
Pick the integration guide that matches your tech stack and follow the step-by-step instructions.
